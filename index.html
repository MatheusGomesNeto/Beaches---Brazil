Wonderful.
Beaches

Brazil is home to some of the most stunning beaches in the world. Here are 10 reasons why you should explore five of these beautiful beaches, that offer a mix of natural beauty, adventure, relaxation, and cultural experiences that make them must-visit destinations for anyone seeking a memorable beach getaway.

São Paulo.
Ilha Bela

Ilhabela, located in São Paulo, Brazil, is a coastal paradise that captivates visitors with its natural beauty and serene atmosphere. Known as the "Beautiful Island," it lives up to its name with its pristine beaches, lush forests, and captivating charm.

Rio de Janeiro.
Copacabana

Copacabana Beach in Rio de Janeiro: Known for its vibrant atmosphere and iconic black and white tiled promenade, Copacabana Beach offers breathtaking views of the city skyline and the sparkling Atlantic Ocean.

Maceió.
Ponta Verde

Ponta Verde Beach is a stunning coastal paradise located in Maceió, Brazil. With its crystal-clear turquoise waters, pristine white sand, and breathtaking natural beauty, it is a true gem for beach lovers and nature enthusiasts.

Alagoas.
Maragogi

Praia de Maragogi in Alagoas: With its crystal-clear, warm waters and extensive coral reefs, Maragogi Beach is a paradise for snorkeling and diving. Take a boat tour to the famous natural pools and witness the colorful underwater world.
